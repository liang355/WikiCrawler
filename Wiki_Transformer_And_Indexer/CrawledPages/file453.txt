<p><b>Kaufbeuren</b> (<small>German pronunciation:</small> <span title="Representation in the International Phonetic Alphabet (IPA)" class="IPA"><a href="/wiki/Help:IPA/Standard_German" title="Help:IPA/Standard German">[kaʊfˈbɔʏʁən]</a></span>) is an <a href="/wiki/Independent_city" title="Independent city">independent town</a> in the <i><a href="/wiki/Regierungsbezirk" title="Regierungsbezirk">Regierungsbezirk</a></i> of <a href="/wiki/Swabia_(Bavaria)" title="Swabia (Bavaria)">Swabia</a>, <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavaria</a>. The town is completely <a href="/wiki/Enclave" class="mw-redirect" title="Enclave">enclaved</a> within the <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Germany" title="Districts of Germany">district</a> of <a href="/wiki/Ostallg%C3%A4u" title="Ostallgäu">Ostallgäu</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Kaufbeuren_Air_Base">Kaufbeuren Air Base</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaufbeuren&amp;action=edit&amp;section=6" title="Edit section: Kaufbeuren Air Base">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Kaufbeuren_Air_Base" title="Kaufbeuren Air Base">Kaufbeuren Air Base</a> was established as a <a href="/wiki/Luftwaffe" title="Luftwaffe">Luftwaffe</a> station in 1935. When seized by the United States Army in May 1945 at the end of World War II it was discovered to be the final location of <a href="/wiki/Nazi_Party" title="Nazi Party">Nazi Party</a>'s top secret <a href="/wiki/Research_Office_of_the_Reich_Air_Ministry" title="Research Office of the Reich Air Ministry">FA</a> <a href="/wiki/Signals_intelligence" title="Signals intelligence">signals intelligence</a> and <a href="/wiki/Cryptanalysis" title="Cryptanalysis">cryptanalytic</a> agency.<sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup> After serving as a U.S. airbase during the post-war period, the field was ultimately returned to the town, and today serves as a <a href="/wiki/German_Air_Force" title="German Air Force">German Air Force</a> training field.</p>
<h2><span id="Tänzelfest"></span><span class="mw-headline" id="T.C3.A4nzelfest">Tänzelfest</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Kaufbeuren&amp;action=edit&amp;section=7" title="Edit section: Tänzelfest">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="https://de.wikipedia.org/wiki/T%C3%A4nzelfest" class="extiw" title="de:Tänzelfest">Tänzelfest</a> is a festival, which takes place usually at the end of June. Children re-enact the history of the town in traditional costume.<a rel="nofollow" class="external autonumber" href="http://www.bavaria.travel/taenzelfest-historical-festival-in-kaufbeuren-bavaria">[1]</a> There are a lot of events at this time.</p>
