<p><b>The Longwood Medical and Academic Area</b> (also known as <b>Longwood Medical Area</b>, <b>LMA</b>, or simply <b>Longwood</b>) is a medical campus in <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">Boston</a>, <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts" title="Massachusetts">Massachusetts</a>. Flanking Longwood Avenue, as it runs from <a href="/wiki/Huntington_Avenue" title="Huntington Avenue">Huntington Avenue</a> to The Riverway, LMA is adjacent to the <a href="/wiki/Fenway%E2%80%93Kenmore" title="Fenway–Kenmore">Fenway–Kenmore</a>, Audubon Circle, and <a href="/wiki/Mission_Hill,_Boston,_Massachusetts" class="mw-redirect" title="Mission Hill, Boston, Massachusetts">Mission Hill</a> neighborhoods, and the town of <a href="/wiki/Brookline,_Massachusetts" title="Brookline, Massachusetts">Brookline</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup></p>
<p>It is most strongly associated with the <a href="/wiki/Harvard_Medical_School" title="Harvard Medical School">Harvard Medical School</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Harvard_School_of_Public_Health" class="mw-redirect" title="Harvard School of Public Health">Harvard School of Public Health</a>, the <a href="/wiki/Harvard_School_of_Dental_Medicine" title="Harvard School of Dental Medicine">Harvard School of Dental Medicine</a> and other medical facilities such as Harvard's teaching hospitals, but prominent non-Harvard institutions are located there as well, such as <a href="/wiki/Massachusetts_College_of_Pharmacy_and_Health_Sciences" class="mw-redirect" title="Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences">Massachusetts College of Pharmacy and Health Sciences</a>, museums, liberal arts colleges, and research centers.</p>
<p>The Harvard Medical Schools central Quadrangle was designed by <a href="/wiki/Shepley,_Rutan_and_Coolidge" title="Shepley, Rutan and Coolidge">Shepley, Rutan and Coolidge</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Transportation">Transportation</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Longwood_Medical_and_Academic_Area&amp;action=edit&amp;section=3" title="Edit section: Transportation">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>LMA is served by two rail stations at opposite ends of Longwood Avenue: "<a href="/wiki/Longwood_(MBTA_station)" title="Longwood (MBTA station)">Longwood</a>" (on the <a href="/wiki/MBTA" class="mw-redirect" title="MBTA">MBTA</a> Green Line's "D" branch) and "<a href="/wiki/Longwood_Medical_Area_(MBTA_station)" title="Longwood Medical Area (MBTA station)">Longwood Medical Area</a>" (on the "E" branch). Several public bus routes serve the area and commuter rail service is available at nearby <a href="/wiki/Ruggles_(MBTA_station)" title="Ruggles (MBTA station)">Ruggles Station</a>. MASCO offers shuttle buses (generally for affiliated personnel only) around the Longwood Medical Area and between Harvard's Cambridge Campus and the Medical Campus (M2). The M2 shuttle is free for passengers holding a Harvard ID, and tickets can be purchased at <a rel="nofollow" class="external text" href="http://www.masco.org/directions/m2-cambridge-harvard-shuttle#Fare">multiple locations</a>.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Energy">Energy</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Longwood_Medical_and_Academic_Area&amp;action=edit&amp;section=4" title="Edit section: Energy">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
