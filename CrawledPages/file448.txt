<p>The following is a list of the most populated cities in Germany. A German <i>Großstadt</i> is defined as a city with more than 100,000 inhabitants.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup> As of December 31, 2015, 79 cities fulfil this criterion. Four of Germany's <i>Großstädte</i> have more than one million inhabitants.</p>
