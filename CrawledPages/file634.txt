<p><a href="/wiki/Harvard_University" title="Harvard University">Harvard University</a>'s projected expansion of its campus in the <a href="/wiki/Allston" title="Allston">Allston</a> neighborhood of <a href="/wiki/Boston" title="Boston">Boston</a> has been in the planning stages since the 1990s, when the institution began accumulating land near and adjacent to <a href="/wiki/Harvard_Business_School" title="Harvard Business School">Harvard Business School</a> and other existing Harvard facilities in Allston, such as <a href="/wiki/Harvard_Stadium" title="Harvard Stadium">Harvard Stadium</a>.<sup id="cite_ref-1" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-1">[1]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-2" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-2">[2]</a></sup><sup id="cite_ref-3" class="reference"><a href="#cite_note-3">[3]</a></sup></p>
