<p><b>Deggendorf</b> is a <a href="/wiki/Districts_of_Germany" title="Districts of Germany"><i>Landkreis</i> (district)</a> in <a href="/wiki/Bavaria" title="Bavaria">Bavaria</a>, <a href="/wiki/Germany" title="Germany">Germany</a>. It is bordered by (from the north and clockwise) the districts of <a href="/wiki/Regen_(district)" title="Regen (district)">Regen</a>, <a href="/wiki/Freyung-Grafenau" title="Freyung-Grafenau">Freyung-Grafenau</a>, <a href="/wiki/Passau_(district)" title="Passau (district)">Passau</a>, <a href="/wiki/Rottal-Inn" title="Rottal-Inn">Rottal-Inn</a>, <a href="/wiki/Dingolfing-Landau" title="Dingolfing-Landau">Dingolfing-Landau</a> and <a href="/wiki/Straubing-Bogen" title="Straubing-Bogen">Straubing-Bogen</a>.</p>

<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="History">History</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Deggendorf_(district)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=1" title="Edit section: History">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The district was established in 1936. Minor changes of the borders occurred in 1972 and led to the present form of the district.</p>
<h2><span class="mw-headline" id="Geography">Geography</span><span class="mw-editsection"><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">[</span><a href="/w/index.php?title=Deggendorf_(district)&amp;action=edit&amp;section=2" title="Edit section: Geography">edit</a><span class="mw-editsection-bracket">]</span></span></h2>
<p>The <a href="/wiki/Danube" title="Danube">Danube</a> river runs through the district from west to east, dividing it into two unequal regions. North of the Danube the <a href="/wiki/Bavarian_Forest" title="Bavarian Forest">Bavarian Forest</a> rises steeply. In the south there is relatively plain countryside. The <a href="/wiki/Isar" title="Isar">Isar</a> river enters the district in the south and meets the Danube close to the city of <a href="/wiki/Deggendorf" title="Deggendorf">Deggendorf</a>.</p>
